WizPedia

(2012)

No Brake!

(2010)

No Brake! is a fast-paced game of survival on the road. Players control a car driving on a busy highway with the accelerator stuck and with no brakes to slow down. While your speed constantly increases, you must avoid deadly collisions and determined cops who are out to get you.

No Brake! was featured in "New and Noteworthy" and "What's Hot" by Apple in February 2010. It was also ranked as top 168 out of 300,000 apps and games on App Store, top 100 in the Game category and top 7 in the Racing category. It was a joint project with a software developer. I lead the project while I did game design, game graphics, and sound editing. It has been discontinued since 2014.
